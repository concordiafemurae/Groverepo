The 961 thread was updated once more on March 31. The anonymous Finland uploaded a picture of jigsaw pieces along with a PGP-signed message. 
The jigsaw puzzle was eventually found out to be a picture of a street sign of the hamlet of Les Drouilhèdes on road D386. The hamlet is part of the small town of Peyremale, France. The jigsaw puzzle was posted 1 day before the ~ (Silence) video was uploaded to the KGB YouTube. Les Drouilhèdes D386 is also seen in Interconnection.
